Our Story

About Sakori

A Legacy of Grace & Tradition

38+ Years of Excellence

Crafting Elegance Since 1985

Sakori Sarees was founded with a simple vision – to bring the finest, most authentic Indian sarees to the women of Kalyan and beyond. What started as a small family-run shop has blossomed into a beloved destination for saree connoisseurs.

Our journey began when our founder, inspired by his grandmother's love for traditional weaves, decided to curate a collection that celebrates India's rich textile heritage. Today, we proudly serve three generations of families who trust us for quality, authenticity, and affordability.
